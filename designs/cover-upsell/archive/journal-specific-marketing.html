Frequently asked questions

Are their any requirements for the image?

We require images that:

  • You own the copyright to or have obtained permission to use
    • Note for images with identifiable people – additional clearance is required and that clearance must specify that the image will be used on the cover
  • Relate to the research discussed in the featured article

Are there any restrictions on the images I can provide for the cover?

We are not able to accept:

  • Images containing any objectification, sexualization, stereotyping, or discrimination of people
  • Images containing inappropriate use, representation, or depiction of religious figures or imagery, and iconography
  • Images that include third-party company names, logos, watermarks, or copyright/trademark symbols
  • Portraits

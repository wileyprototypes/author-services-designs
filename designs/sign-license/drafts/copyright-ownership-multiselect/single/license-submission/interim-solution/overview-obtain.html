Please upload and submit the signed license to publish your article successfully

1. Obtain license

NIST have asked Wiley to instruct you to obtain the NIST cover sheet from your department. Your progress has been saved and you can return to this point at any time.

2. Signing instructions
  1. Review the Wiley "Copyright Transfer Agreement"
    • The Copyright Transfer Agreement does not need to be signed or uploaded
  2. Request "NIST" cover sheet from your agency.
  3. Add the following text to section 6 in the "NIST" cover sheet:

    By signing this Publication Form, the NIST Author(s) or Editor, on behalf of NIST, agree to the provisions set out below, which modify and supersede, solely with respect to NIST, any conflicting provisions that are in the Publisher’s standard copyright agreement (the “Publisher’s Agreement”). If a Publisher’s Agreement is attached, execution of this Publication Form constitutes an execution of the Publisher’s Agreement, subject to the provisions and conditions of this Publication Form.

  4. You and an authorized representitive both sign the "NIST" cover sheet
  5. Ensure any signed documents are in one of the following formats ready for upload:
    • .pdf
    • .png
    • .jpg
    • .jpeg
3. Upload the signed license

Drag and drop your files or click to upload

You are able to upload up to 10 files. Please make sure that they are in the correct order by using the up and down arrows. Total size for all files should not exceed 30 MiB. Supported file types are jpeg, jpg, png, pdf.
